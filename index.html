Trio of Love Missteps


In the moonlight's soft glow, a chance encounter unfolds

In the "Prelude to a Misstep", a story yet untold

However, ill-timed words turn love's beginning into uncertainty

Leading to a dance on the bridge of what could be


In the "Dance of Uncertainty", we twirl and sway

Navigating feelings that we're both afraid to say

Now, in the "Midnight Echoes of Regret" we reflect

On love's missteps and choices, shrouded in the darkness of regret
